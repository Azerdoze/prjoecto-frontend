What is it?

Welcome to the fantasy setting of Arthan Uthyl. Arthan Uthyl is a fantasy campaign setting heavily inspired on historical ancient civilizations and that has been mutating since it's creation, in 2004. With it I want to create a live experience, where players can feel part of a living world where there action help shape the next eras of the fiction.

Who is behind it?

My name is Eurico Teles, portuguese and with wordly experience, and I have been a Dungeon Master for Dungeons & Dragons (v 3.5) since 2001, and Pathfinder (v 1.0) since it's release. Both a fantasy and sci-fi fan, I have always been fascinated with this creative worlds and trying to create one has been a life goal of mine since very young. When I started playing D&D I finally had that opportunity and the feedback that I got from players was fantastic. So I decided it was time to make it big and digitalize it.

Why now?

In 2015 I tried learning by myself web developing and release my first website, a prototype of this one. Though truth be told, I didn't know much. Since then I have embarked on an actual course and though it would be a nice challenge to combine these two things that I love and recreate the old website but with nowadays knowledge and technology.

Can I be part of it?

Sure you can! on each section of the website you can find a comments section where you can leave a suggestion, an appreciation or a note. Please be respectful since this is an independent project and I am by no means a professional fantasy writer.

Can I use your setting?

That's the purpose of it being online. While it's technically made to be used with Pathfinder, feel free to use it in whichever fantasy tabletop RPG you see fit and share your experiences with it. I will be happy to hear about it.
